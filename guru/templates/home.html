Why Guru

Less 'teching', more 'teaching'.

GURU is designed to be extremely simple, inituitive and easy to use. You can get started with it in the blink of an eye. So as a teacher, rather than getting distracted by technicalities. You can focus on what you love:teaching.

Handing out notes is as easy as sending an email

Send out handouts, notes, and even textbooks to as many students you want, just with press of one button.

Know how every student is doing at a glance.

Easily keep track of every student who is in your classroom. You can grade their assignments, contact them directly from within the website, post comments. Providing a personalizing touch to the process of academic instruction may no longer remain a dream.

    For Teachers

  • ❯ You can create classroom with unique entry protocols that you can then distribute amongst your students who want to join.
  • ❯ You can provide assignment and set deadlines.
  • ❯ You can view students who had submitted on time, Who couldn't, who did and who didn't.
  • ❯ You can add resources in classroom and also resource hub.
  • ❯ You can authorize students to post in classroom.
  • ❯ You can contact students directly from the webapp.
  • ❯ You can add as many students as you want in classroom.
  • ❯ You can create polls and authorize who can participate in polls

    For Students

  • ❯ You can join in any classroom with the unique classroom code provided.
  • ❯ You can submit your assignments and classwork without breaking a sweat.
  • ❯ You can contact your teachers directly from the webapp.
  • ❯ Switching between classroom and subjects is seamless.
